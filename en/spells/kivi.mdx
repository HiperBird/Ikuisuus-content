# Kivi

With a grasping motion you summon a small pebble. The source of all great inventions.

> **Kivi**  
> _Transmutation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 24h
>  
> Conjure three pebbles and imbue them with magic. You can make a ranged spell attack with one of the pebbles. If thrown, a pebble has a range of 30 feet. On a hit, the target takes bludgeoning damage equal to 1d6 + your spellcasting ability modifier. Whether the attack hits or misses, the pebble loses its magical properties.
> 
> If you cast this spell again, all your existing pebbles vanish
> 
> For each, choose among the following additional effects for how to enchant it:
> 
> **Brilliant** - This stone has an aura that emits bright light in a 20 foot radius. When thrown, it will stop shining, however, if it hits a target or medium sized item, it will transfer its aura to it, and it will keep shining for a minute.
> 
> **Seeking** - Instead of making a ranged spell attack, force the target to make a Dexterity saving throw, ignoring cover.
> 
> **Bouncing** - While this stone hits, it leaps to a new target of your choice within 10 feet of the last target. Make an attack roll against the new target, and if it hits, make a new damage roll dealing 1d6 less damage than the last bounce. Once the attack deals no d6 damage, it can't bounce.
> 
> **Far** - this stone can be thrown 30 feet further.
> 
> **Brutal** - This stone isn't thrown, but instead used as a blunt weapon. make a melee spell attack against a weapon within 5 feet. If it hits, you may add your strength to the damage.
> 
> This spell summons an additional stone and its damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level, 11th level, and 17th level.
>
