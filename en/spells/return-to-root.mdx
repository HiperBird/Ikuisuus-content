# Return to Root

The wood of your club or quarterstaff thickens and hardens, humming with blunt, living force.

---

> **Return to Root**  
> _Transmutation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a club or quarterstaff)  
> **Duration**: 1 minute  
>  
>The wood of a club or quarterstaff you are holding is imbued with nature’s power. For the duration, you can use your spellcasting ability instead of Strength for the attack and damage rolls of melee attacks using that weapon, and the weapon’s damage die becomes a d8.  
>
>Additionally, you can bash it against dirt or stone as an action for the following effects:
> 
>- If you hit an area of loose earth, you can instantaneously excavate it, move it along the ground, and deposit it up to 5 feet away. This movement doesn’t have enough force to cause damage.
>
>- If the dirt or stone you target is on the ground, you cause it to become difficult terrain. Alternatively, you can cause the ground to become normal terrain if it is already difficult terrain. This change lasts for 1 hour.  You can have no more than two of this areas active at a time, and you can dismiss them as an action.
>
